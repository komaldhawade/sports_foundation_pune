Projects

Young Spirits Initiative

To make sports more enjoyable for diabetes type 1 patients and students, we are planning to redesign some of the games such that their benets are reaped without them being too exhaustive or painful for the participants. Through this project, we hope to introduce kids to sports and show how important it is for their growth both physically and mentally

Happy Feet Initiative

The Happy Feet Initiative: With this initiative, we hope to foster relationships between parents and their kids through a variety of traditional and modern activities that we will plan, all with the goal of spending quality time together away from technology. We want the parents to reminisce their good old days of childhood playing with their own parents in the absence of technology, so that they can create the same memories with their own children by spending a gadget-free day and enjoying a digital detox.
